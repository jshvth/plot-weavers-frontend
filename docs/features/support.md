# Support & Contact

## Summary
Users can send a support request through a contact form powered by EmailJS. A toast confirms success or failure.

## User Flow
- Fill out the contact form on `/support`.
- Submit to send the email via EmailJS.
- Receive a success or error toast notification.

## Key Behaviors
- Email field is validated by a simple pattern.
- Toast auto-dismisses after a short delay.
